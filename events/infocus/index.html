In Focus

    Join 'In Focus,' a celebration of visual storytelling through photography. Showcase your creative talents and capture unique moments.

    Date : 14th October

    Time : 10:00 AM ~ 12:00 PM





    This is an Individual participant event.

    To participate in this event we have some rules over there



  • Each Participants must Submit two Photos for the Event.

  • Photo 1 (Raw): Submit the first photo in its raw form.

  • Photo 2 (Edited): The second photo should be edited.

  • Theme: Participants are required to align their photos with one of the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), known as SDG 17. These goals are part of the United Nations' 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development. Each photo should clearly relate to and represent one specific SDG.

  • As part of their participation in the challenge, participants must follow the Instagram pages infyra23, sairamit_it, and hackinhub23.
