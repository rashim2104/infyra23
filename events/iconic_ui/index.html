Iconic U & I

    "Designers! Get ready to showcase your design prowess at 'Iconic U&I'! This event challenges you to create visually captivating UIs for products and set new standards in user experience."

    Date : 14th October

    Time : 10:00 AM ~ 12:00 PM





    Team Composition

  • Each team can consist maximum of 2 members.

  • No solo participants or larger teams are allowed.

  • The event is open to all students from engineering and arts and science colleges.

  • Please ensure that you meet this eligibility requirement to participate.



      • Round Structure

  • The event will consist of 2 rounds.

  • Details of each round, including the format and objectives, will be communicated to participants before the event begins.



      • Materials and Resources

  • Participants must bring their own laptops and Ethernet cables.

  • All other event materials will be provided by the organizers unless stated otherwise.



      • Time Management

  • Participants must adhere to the time limits set for each round.

  • Failure to do so may result in penalties.



      • We are eagerly waiting to see you all in the event.
