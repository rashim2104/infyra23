Innovate Chatter-Ji

    Get ready to tackle challenges head-on at 'Innovate Chatter-Ji.' This event is all about quick thinking and rapid problem-solving. Join us to unleash your innovation in the heat of the moment!

    Date : 14th October

    Time : 1:00 PM ~ 2:00 PM





    This is a Team event.

    To participate in this event we have some rules over there



  • Each team can consist maximum of 2 members.

  • The event is open to all students from engineering and arts and science colleges.

  • Please ensure that you meet this eligibility requirement to participate.

  • This game consists of Two Rounds


      • Round Structure

  • Round 1 : Participants will receive problem statements and form teams of 2 to brainstorm innovative solutions. Within a designated timeframe, they will analyze and propose solutions to the given problems.

  • Round 2 : teams will verbally present their innovative solutions to the assigned problems within a 5-minute time limit. Presentations should be clear and concise, addressing the problem statement and proposed solution without the use of PowerPoint slides.




    We are eagerly waiting to see you all in the event.
